Hi, welcome to my website / portfolio.

My name is Alejandro Bermúdez Pascual. I was qualified as Architect in November 2016 at the university of Granada, Spain. I came to the UK looking for new oportunities and have been already more than four years working in Cardiff, Wales, where i am currently based.

I consider myself a curious and proactive person and I am always looking to improve my abilities in different fields. I use to aim towards the efficency, either at work and my personal life, and I take it as a goal when facing new challenges. I have a good management under pressure and an excellent learning curve.

#Reliable #Efficent #Adaptable #Proactive #Trustworthy #HighAttentionToDetail #Creative #InteriorAndExteriorDetailingKnowledge


2007
Two months in summer as general laborer at RINCOLOMA S.L.
I was doing all kind of tasks under the supervision of the construction manager / site manager. It gave me a starting point and a helpful insight about construction site issues, queries and situations. The concrete structure of a block of apartments was being done. During this period I helped with the steel reinforcement of the floor slab preparation. Also doing basics as washing croncrete slabs, cleaning dust, preparing mortar, carrying bricks...
2007 / 2016
University period at the ETSAG (Escuela Técnica Superior de Arquiectura de Granada)
In September 2007 I moved from Málaga to Granada to start studying Architecture.
2008
One and a half months in summer as general laborer at RINCOLOMA S.L.
The summer after the first shocked year I had the chance to continue working in the same building. At this time the whole structure was already done and they had been starting with the external and internal partitions (traditional brick). It helped me to understand another part of the process on site as I was also helping to define walls location, preparing mortar and cleaning surfaces.
2009
One months in summer as general laborer at RINCOLOMA S.L.

During this period the building I have had been working before was almost finished so I just had to help with some cleaning in it.

I was moved to do some reparations to a pool on a smaller building. We had to remove a wall of concrete blocks and redo it adding new perforations to the concrete foot to stick with epoxy resin some more reinforcements. After that we reamended the cobblestones paving next to it.

2012
Workshop "Urban, social and environmental actions in the north area of Granada" (1st edition)
This was a workshop of 35 hours where we got in touch with some conflitive areas in Granada. It was focused on analise and expose issues and proposals for / with the residents.
Workshop "Derivas" as part of one of the core subjects.
This workshop was to awake different ways of looking at projects. We had to go to another city, find a place for a project and do a proposal. All of this was taking into account not only the locals needs but also the whole process as an architectural strategy to understand the place.
2012 / 2013
Nine months Erasmus program at the ENSAP (École nationale supérieure d'architecture) in Bordeaux, France.
The main experience after nine months studying in France was to understand a different way of thinking about / doing architecture. All the projects I was involved had a strong urbanistic view.
2014
Workshop "Building devices to energize the Beiro river in the north of Granada"
After the first workshop done in 2012 in this area analising and documenting some issues and possibles actions in this north area we started producing and helping the locals to build allotments and green spaces for the whole neighbourhood on the sides of a small river. We built some artefacts with natural and recycled materials.
2016
Degree's final project
"A cemetery in Granada" was the topic I choose to work on.
2017
Workshop "Eco-Furniture and creative recycling" for NESIForum2017
This was a workshop to propose and create the furniture for the New Economy and Social Innovation forum held in the congress center of Málaga from 19th to 22th April. We had to build the scenery of the event.
